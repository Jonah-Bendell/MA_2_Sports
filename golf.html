The EPS Golf team

    • The Eastside Preparatory School (EPS) golf team is known for its outstanding skill and sportsmanship on the course. With a strong tradition of excellence, the team consistently demonstrates dedication, teamwork, and a passion for the game. EPS golfers are recognized for their impressive performances in competitions, often achieving top placements and representing their school with pride. Their commitment to practice and improvement has made them one of the most respected teams in the league , inspiring both teammates and future players.
